Goal: Find the optimal investment allocation that maximizes the annualized average return while keeping the portfolio risk below the threshold (15%). The initial investment is USD 10k.
 
Formula:
Annualized Average Return = (1 + avg_return)^(1/number_of_period) - 1 
Portfolio Risk: sqrt( sum( (individual_stock_risk^2) * (investment_allocation) ) )
